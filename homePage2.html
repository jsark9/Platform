Rezzo's vision is to help individuals meet their short and long-term career goals. Creating and excelling in your career is hard; here at Rezzio, we are committed to lifelong learning. Learning outside of the classroom, the skills you need to excel your career.


Our mission is to help individuals and college students determine their goals and find the path to success. College students can efficiently create their personal brand over four or five years, increasing the chances of a successful and fulfilling career.

WHY REZZIO?

STUDENT

Rezzio gives students the opportunity to understand their skills and how they relate to their career. Rezzio is the place to start, we identify students skills and find their career path. Rezzio's career pathway focuses on four areas of career success or WACE.

ADVISOR

Rezzio helps career advisors manage their students. We provide an overall vision of student activities through which you can easily figure out who needs help in what areas. Rezzio helps advisors build personal interaction between themselves and their student.
